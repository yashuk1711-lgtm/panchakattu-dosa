THE_STORY

Named after
a man in
a dhoti.

Panchakattu means dhoti in Telugu. The founder, an IT manager who quit his job for dosas, learned the craft from a nameless dosa maker in Tadipatri — a man known only by what he wore. The shack had no name, so this one carries his.

The menu stays deliberately small. No 60-item list. Just Rayalaseema-style dosas, idlys and pongal, done the way they're done back home — generous neyyi, proper kaaram, served on a leaf.
